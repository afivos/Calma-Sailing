  • Calma is a Jeanneau Sun Odyssey 490, the flagship of the latest generation of Sun Odyssey models. She is built in 2020 in high standards from the French leading shipbuilding company Jeanneau, achieving the most comfort and safe experience on board.

    The 4+1 cabins and 4+1 bathrooms arrangement offers unique comfort and privacy to all the guests while a professional skipper can be provided without any spatial compromise thanks to the independant skipper cabin and WC on the front. Due to the inclined side decks, circulation from deck to cockpit has never been easier and safer. The folded bathing platform on the back extends the outdoor living area and brings you closer to the water while anchored. A very balanced and innovative design that satisfies even the most demanding sailors.

  • Check weather forecast daily before sailing.

    Don't forget to bring your suncream and sunglasses.

    A sweater and a light wind jacket is always necessary.

    It is strongly advised that you only bring soft-sided, non-wheeled luggage, and wear flat shoes when onboard.
